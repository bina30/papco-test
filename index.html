Papco Business Services

Papco corporate services specializes in the sale of hygiene and consumables to companies and institutions. Papco Business Services' staff strives to provide personal and professional advice tailored to the needs of each company.

We at Papco Corporate Services specialize in the sale of hygiene and consumables to general companies and institutions. We also offer a wide range of products for restaurants and hotels. We are a party to Ríkiskaup's framework agreements in hygiene products and plastic bags and therefore handle sales and services to many government agencies and municipalities.

In our product range you can find toilet paper, napkins, cleaning products, plastic bags, disposable packaging, hotel products, fundraising products and much more that can be viewed and ordered in our online store.
